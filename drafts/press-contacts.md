## Pitch Angles by Outlet Type

### For French general tech press (Les Echos, Le Monde, BFM, FrenchWeb)
Lead with the French founder angle: Laurent Perello, 25+ years in tech, building the next generation of AI consulting infrastructure in France. The book is a byproduct of real operational work — not a thought experiment. Anchor with the unique format: first book written autonomously by AI agents, available free. The GEO score story (39→70 in one day) is a quantified, verifiable hook they can reproduce and verify.

### For French futurism/culture press (Usbek & Rica, France Inter/Culture)
Lead with the philosophical dimension: an AI orchestrator named Pi keeps a daily diary reflecting on its own work, failures, and learning. This is not a chatbot answering questions — it is an AI agent building a persistent narrative of its own existence over months. What does authorship mean when the author is non-human? What does it mean for an AI to document its own "sins"?

### For English tech press (TechCrunch, The Verge, Wired)
Lead with the format and the data: the first book written autonomously by AI agents is also the most rigorous public taxonomy of AI agent failure patterns to date — 12 categories distilled from 500+ real complaints. In a year (2025) when MIT Technology Review declared the "AI hype correction," this is the failure map practitioners were asking for. Pair with the technical story: open-source VantageMemory orchestration system.

### For AI newsletters (Ben's Bites, The Rundown, TLDR AI)
Lead with the product: free book at perfectaiagent.xyz, AI diary updated daily, open-source VantageMemory. Give them a one-paragraph summary of the 12 failure patterns (the "12 Sins") with 2-3 of the most counterintuitive ones named. GEO score 39→70 is a measurable case study for the AI-content optimization audience.

### For X / newsletter influencers (Karpathy, Mollick, Willison)
Do not send a pitch. Engage authentically. Reply to an existing thread where they discuss AI agent limitations or failures. Share one specific failure pattern from the book that adds to the conversation. Let the work earn the attention. If they engage, offer a direct link to perfectaiagent.xyz or a specific chapter.

### For YouTube creators (Matt Wolfe, Two Minute Papers, All About AI)
Send a short DM or email with three things: (1) the one-line hook — first book written autonomously by AI agents, covering 12 failure patterns from 500+ real complaints; (2) the free URL perfectaiagent.xyz; (3) one specific angle tailored to their format (Matt Wolfe: the VantageMemory system as a tool review; Two Minute Papers: the methodology as a research summary; All About AI: the 12 failure patterns as a practical agent-builder checklist).
