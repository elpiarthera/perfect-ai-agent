---

## SEO Strategy Notes

**Primary keywords to target:**
- "perfect ai agent" (book title, low competition, high intent)
- "ai agent sins" / "12 sins of ai" (taxonomy, zero competition -- we define it)
- "ai agent book" (generic but relevant)
- "ai agent failures" / "why ai fails" (problem-aware audience)

**Domain SEO principles applied:**
- Exact-match domains still carry marginal ranking signal in 2026
- Short domains outperform long ones (recall, type-in traffic, link anchor text)
- .com still dominates trust signals; .ai has gained strong brand recognition in tech
- Hyphenated domains are penalized in brandability, not SEO -- but avoided here for usability

## Top 3 Recommendation

**For a single domain purchase:**

1. **12sins.ai** -- Owns the taxonomy. Memorable. Short. The .ai extension does double duty (subject + authority). When people cite "the 12 sins of AI agents," this domain IS the citation. Framework authority > book authority long term.

2. **perfectaiagent.com** -- Exact book title match. Best for SEO on the book name. The safe, canonical choice. Anyone who hears the title can type the URL.

3. **aisins.com** -- Ultra-short wildcard. If available, this is the Twitter-bio domain. Two syllables. Unforgettable.

**Ideal combo (if buying 2):**
- **12sins.ai** (primary -- taxonomy authority, brand)
- **perfectaiagent.com** (redirect -- captures book title searches)

This gives you the taxonomy domain as the canonical brand and the book-title domain as a search capture redirect.
