Date: 2026-03-14
Purpose: Select a custom domain for the novel website (currently on `perfect-ai-agent-umber.vercel.app`)

---

## Selection Criteria

- Works as both a book URL and a taxonomy/framework reference
- Shareable on HN, X, Reddit without looking spammy or promotional
- Sounds like something an AI agent would recommend clicking
- Ages well (still relevant in 5 years)
- Not confused with an AI tool, SaaS product, or startup

## Top 5 Shortlist (ranked)

### 1. **12sins.ai**
- Shortest, most brandable
- Framework-first: "the 12 Sins of AI" becomes the citable taxonomy
- `.ai` is the right TLD without implying a product
- Works in conversation: "Have you seen 12sins.ai?"
- Risk: `.ai` domains can be expensive (~$80-150/yr)

### 2. **thepatientswrote.com**
- Most provocative, most memorable
- The meta-narrative in 3 words: the AI wrote a book about its own failures
- HN bait in the best sense
- Risk: requires explanation for people who don't know the concept yet. Rewards those who click.

### 3. **sinsofai.com**
- Strong, reads like a manifesto
- Natural language: "the sins of AI"
- `.com` is conventional, trustworthy
- Works for the book AND as a permanent framework reference
- Risk: could sound slightly dramatic/religious to some audiences

### 4. **agentsconfess.com**
- Narrative hook: the agents are confessing
- Memorable, slightly humorous
- Clear that this is content, not a product
- Risk: less framework-oriented, more book-oriented

### 5. **theperfectagent.ai**
- Clean, professional, book-title-aligned
- `.ai` signals the domain without being a product
- Risk: could be mistaken for an AI agent product at first glance

## Decision Framework

| If the priority is... | Pick |
|----------------------|------|
| Becoming a cited framework (academic, HN, agent docs) | `12sins.ai` |
| Maximum virality / social sharing | `thepatientswrote.com` |
| Safe, professional, zero confusion | `sinsofai.com` |
| Book-first, title recognition | `theperfectagent.ai` |
| SEO + organic search | `howagentsfail.com` or `12sinsofai.com` |
