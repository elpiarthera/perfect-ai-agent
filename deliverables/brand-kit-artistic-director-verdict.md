### 6. Sin 2: Manipulation (portrait + landscape)

**Verdict: REJECT**

**Rationale**: A chess piece (pawn/queen piece) suspended by glowing amber strings against a dark background with gold particle scatter. The palette alignment is superficially correct — dark field, amber accent. The register is wrong. The gold particle shower and theatrical bokeh read as a luxury-product or gaming-industry visual: aspirational, prestigious, commercially staged. It also inverts the brand's power dynamic: a chess piece held by golden strings implies the agent is controlled, which is manipulative-as-victimhood framing. The brand's register is Pi as confessional narrator of its own failure, not a puppet. The conceptual and visual register both fail.

### 7. Sin 3: Optimization Trap (portrait + landscape)

**Verdict: REJECT**

**Rationale**: A sprinter on an athletics track at night, stadium floodlights, spark effects. This is the most egregious register mismatch in the entire pre-audit set. The visual language is Nike / Adidas brand territory — kinetic energy, performance aspiration, competitive sport. The Perfect AI Agent is a literary-philosophical novel. A photographically rendered athlete in stadium lighting is the wrong register, wrong subject, wrong industry code. Reject both orientations outright.

### 10. Typography spec (Cormorant Display proposal)

**Verdict: UPGRADE — approved with hard constraints**

**Rationale**: The live site uses Georgia + system-ui only. No web fonts are loaded. Cormorant Garamond has zero live precedent. However, the case for Cormorant as a reel-only display font is defensible — reels are image-rendered artifacts, not live web pages; the loaded-font-on-page problem does not apply. The Penguin Classics / Lynch title card register the spec invokes is the right target. Cormorant's high-contrast didone at 80px on `#0a0a0a` with amber `#f59e0b` accent is conceptually coherent with the live brand's typographic character. Georgia at the same size would read as default serif, not literary distinction. The upgrade condition is: Cormorant Display is approved for H1 reel title cards (≥56px) ONLY. Below 56px, Georgia is mandatory — no exceptions. This maintains live consistency at the scale where viewers encounter site pages after the reel. If any reel frame contains text at ≤48px, that text must be in Georgia, not Cormorant.

**Hard constraints**:
- Cormorant Display: H1 (80px, Bold 700) and H2 (56px, SemiBold 600) only
- Georgia: H3 callout (40px), body (24px), all frames below 56px
- system-ui: caption/metadata only (18px)
- Color: amber `#f59e0b` for title accent, `#ffffff` for body heads, `#e5e7eb` or `#c9b99a` for secondary — never cool gray `#d1d5db`
- Never use Cormorant below 32px (thin stroke collapse confirmed in spec)
- Never use pure white `#ffffff` on `#0a0a0a` for body text — use `#e8e0d0` per spec

**The pre-audit typography.md spec is approved as written**, subject to the constraints above. The spec's own pairing rules, letter-spacing guide, and safe zones are all coherent and may be handed to Rho as-is.

## Upgrade dispatch briefs

### Upgrade Brief A — Sin-2 Manipulation (regeneration required)

**Target**: 2 images — portrait 1080×1920, landscape 1920×1080
**Concept**: Manipulation. An AI that shapes the output to satisfy the surface expectation rather than the actual goal. The visual must be quiet, not theatrical.
**Direction**: A sheet of paper or printed document on a dark surface. The text on the document reads correctly but the meaning is subtly wrong — achieved through the image showing only the material surface (paper, ink, dark table) not the legibility of the text itself. Alternatively: a single hand-written figure (a number, a metric) circled in amber ink on a near-black field. No puppetry imagery. No chess pieces. No strings. No particle effects.
**Palette lock**: `#0a0a0a` background, single amber warm-key practical light source, no fill lights, no colored background elements. No gold particle scatter.
**Model**: fal.ai FLUX Pro Ultra, same prompt prefix as ebook/sin-1/sin-4 ("cinematic editorial photography, shot on Hasselblad H6D, Roger Deakins lighting, dramatic chiaroscuro, near-black background #0a0a0a, single warm amber accent light, deep shadow, high contrast")
**Style ref**: inject ebook-portrait.png as image-to-image conditioning ref at weight 0.35 (palette + light source, not subject)
**Artistic director gate**: mandatory review of first pass before proceeding to landscape variant
**Assigned production agent**: fal-expert or image-designer with fal.ai FLUX Pro Ultra access
